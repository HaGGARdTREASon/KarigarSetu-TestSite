About Us

At KarigarSetu, we’re on a heartfelt mission to uplift local artisans by bridging the gap between traditional craftsmanship and modern technology.

Across India, countless talented karigars (artisans) create exquisite handmade products — each piece a reflection of heritage and dedication. Yet, many struggle to market their work in today’s digital world.

KarigarSetu acts as the bridge, empowering artisans with powerful tools to simplify marketing and take control of their businesses:

  • Product Name Generator: Create elegant, market-friendly names that capture the essence of your craft.
  • Price Suggestion Engine: Fair and competitive pricing based on craftsmanship and trends.
  • SEO & Tagline Optimizer: Smart suggestions for keywords, tags, and descriptions to make your work stand out online.

We aim to take Indian craftsmanship global — one handmade product at a time.
